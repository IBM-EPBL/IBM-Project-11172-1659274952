Symptoms

In general, signs and symptoms of arrhythmias may include:

  1. Feeling of skipped heartbeat
  2. Dizziness or feeling lightheaded.
  3. Shortness of breath.
  4. Chest discomfort.
  5. Weakness or fatigue
  6. Weakening of the heart muscle or low ejection fraction.
  7. Anxiety
  8. Sweating
  9. A racing heartbeat (tachycardia)
  10. A slow heartbeat (bradycardia)
