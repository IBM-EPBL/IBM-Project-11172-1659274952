ECG Arrhythmia classification

A heart arrhythmia is an irregular heartbeat. Heart rhythm problems (heart arrhythmias) occur
when the electrical signals that coordinate the heart's beats don't work properly.
The faulty signaling causes the heart to beat too fast (tachycardia), too slow (bradycardia) or irregularly.
Heart arrhythmias may feel like a fluttering or racing heart and may be harmless.
A heart-healthy lifestyle can help prevent heart damage that can trigger certain heart arrhythmias.
